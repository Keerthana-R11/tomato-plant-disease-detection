1. Why is early plant disease detection important?

Early detection helps farmers take timely action, reducing crop loss, improving yield, and minimizing pesticide use — which supports both economic and environmental goals.

2. What are common methods for plant disease detection?

Manual inspection
Image processing techniques
Machine learning (e.g., SVM, KNN)
Deep learning (CNNs, transfer learning)
Remote sensing with drones or satellites
